# HashiCorp Certified: Terraform Associate (004) Notes

## General Information

- **Exam Duration:** 1 hour
- **Question Count:** 50-60 questions
- **Tested on:** Terraform 1.12
- **Certification Expiration:** 2 years
- **Cost:** $70.50 USD

## Question Types

- True/False
- Multiple Choice
- Multiple Choice - Multiple Answer
- Fill-in the blank
